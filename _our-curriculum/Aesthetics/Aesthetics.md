<p><strong>Aesthetics Goal: <u>Confident Practising Talents, Lifelong Learners</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Students to be able to</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>enjoy the arts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop awareness and appreciation of the arts in local and global cultures</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>gain insights to other cultures, understand the role of the arts in the
culture and in the society and then make meaning through connecting with
society and culture</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>be open to the different types of arts&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop capacity to critically discern and process visual information&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop ability for creative expression and effective visual communication
through the arts</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Key Beliefs in the Arts Education</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The power of the arts in nurturing Innovative Thinkers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The importance of exposure to nurture Global Citizens who are appreciative
of the arts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The value of platforms in the grooming of talented individuals as Lifelong
Learners</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Quality of performance of Confident Leaders improves with exposure to
quality experiences</p>
</li>
</ul>
