<h4><strong>Embark on Your PPS Adventure: Reconnect. Reminisce. Renew.</strong></h4>
<p>As you step into our homecoming, collect your exclusive Exploration Map
at the registration counter - your key to reconnecting with our school.</p>
<p>Your journey of discovery awaits:</p>
<p>*Try your hand in Applied Problem-based learning on GreeNovation our STEM
Hub</p>
<p>*Learn about how PPS develops the Growth Mindset in our Punggolites at
the Care Hub</p>
<p>*Renew your love for reading in our revitalised Library</p>
<p>*Celebrate our PPS heritage at the Heritage &amp; Cultural Hub</p>
<p>*Experience our champion mindset in action at the Areana and Parade Square</p>
<p>Complete your exploration by collecting stamps at each discovery point.
This will earn you a limited-edition PPS 30th Anniversary collectable -
a special memento of our shared journey.</p>
<p>Throughout the day, let our talented performers remind you of our enduring
Pride in Performance as they showcase artistic talents.</p>
<p>Whether you're reconnecting with old friends, reminiscing about cherished
memories, or renewing your connection with PPS, every corner holds a discovery
waiting to be made. And don't forget to reminisce over familiar tastes
at our canteen food or enjoy the pop up candy floss, popcorn or ice cream
carts!</p>
